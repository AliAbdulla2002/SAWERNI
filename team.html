Our Team

Meet the team behind SAWERNI

Supervisor

DR. GHASSAN MOHAMMED ANWAR AL KOUREITI

Assistant Professor - Information Systems

Dr. Ghassan Alkoureiti is an Assistant Professor of Information Systems at the University of Bahrain, specializing in advanced IS courses and curriculum development. He has coordinated the internship program and supervised numerous senior projects. His research interests include enterprise systems, IT governance, blockchain, socio-material IS practices, and game development. He holds certifications in CEH, PCAP, and COBIT5, and earned a BSc from the University of Bahrain (2009), an MSc from George Washington University (2013), and a PhD from the University of Lancaster (2018).

Student

ALI JAAFAR ABDALI ALASFOOR

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

Ali, an Information Systems student at the University of Bahrain, was the Team Leader for SAWERNI, a photography and videography platform for the local market, developed for the ITIS499 capstone project using FlutterFlow and Firebase. He coordinated the project lifecycle, focusing on features like AI Auto Captioning, AI Dynamic Categorization, and AI Content Moderation. Ali implemented a non-addictive gamification system to enhance user engagement and employed Agile SDLC methodologies for project management, ensuring scalability and functionality to support Bahrain's media industry.

Student

HASAN ALI JASIM ALI

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

Hasan is a Computer Science/Information Systems student at the University of Bahrain and served as the backbone of the SAWERNI platform's technical infrastructure. He was responsible for designing and managing the Firebase backend, architecting the platform's full data schema — covering critical collections including Users, Posts, Comments, Events, Chats, ChatMessages, User Stats, and User Points. His database design ensured data integrity, scalability, and efficient retrieval across the entire platform. As System Analyst, Hasan led the requirements elicitation process, translating stakeholder needs into well-defined functional and non-functional requirements. He also produced key system models including Data Flow Diagrams (DFDs) and the Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD). On the quality assurance side, he designed and executed unit testing and functional testing plans, ensuring SAWERNI met its reliability and performance standards before delivery.

Student

ALI SALEH MAHDI ABDULLA

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

Ali Saleh is a Computer Science/Information Systems student at the University of Bahrain who shaped the look, feel, and user experience of the SAWERNI platform. He was responsible for designing and implementing the platform's full interface using FlutterFlow, crafting screens across all major modules — including Login & Registration, Home Feed, Post Creation, Bookmarks, Comments, Chat & Messaging, Events, Explore & Search, User Profiles, Followers/Following, Gamification, and Settings. His UI work emphasized a non-distractive, clean aesthetic that sets SAWERNI apart from noisy social media platforms. Ali Saleh also contributed to backend integration, connecting UI components with Firebase in real time, and participated in UI consistency testing to ensure a seamless and polished experience across all screens.
